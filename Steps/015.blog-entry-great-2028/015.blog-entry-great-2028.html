Title: Thank you for a great 2028!

Author: Phil Terry

Tags: wrapup, announcement

Created: 10/1/2028

What can we say? It was another awesome year, so many of you overcome apprehensions and dove right into being amazing. I always find myself in awe of just how much we can accomplish when we are put into the right circumstances. MagnifAscent was founded to help me kick my fears, but I learned that there are a lot of us out there that want to push past our barriers and see what’s outside, and that made me realize that I’m always among friends.

So here’s to another year of the great outdoors of the world and our minds, and to the many more to come!
