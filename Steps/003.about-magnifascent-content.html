About MagnifAscent

Welcome to MagnifAscent, one of the world’s premiere extreme tour companies. We were founded over a decade ago and have seen hundreds of Adventurers push their own limitations and come out the other side stronger and more resilient.

Our mission is to help you overcome your limits through physical exertion, beautiful environments, comradery and unusual activities. With nearly a dozen tours every year across several continents, and five unique and exclusive tour types, there is something for almost everybody*.
